Healthy Online Shopping Tips

1. Set a Clear Budget

Before browsing online stores, decide how much you can spend. Planning your budget prevents overspending and helps you stay financially healthy.

2. Avoid Emotional Buying

Don’t shop when you’re stressed, bored, or sad. Emotional purchases often lead to regret later.

3. Wait 24 Hours Before Buying

If the item is not urgent, wait a day before checking out. This helps reduce impulsive decisions.

4. Check Reviews and Ratings

Always read product reviews and check seller ratings to make sure you're getting quality items.

5. Protect Your Personal Information

Use secure payment methods and never share your OTP or sensitive information online.
