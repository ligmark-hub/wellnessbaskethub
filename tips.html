Healthy Online Shopping Tips

1. Implement a "Cool-off" Period.

Wait 24 to 48 hours before clicking "checkout" on non-essential items. This helps ensure the purchase is a rational choice rather than a reaction to a temporary state.

2. Monitor Your "Restfulness" Indicators

Avoid "late-night scrolling". Set a digital curfew to stop shopping at least one hour before bed to protect your sleeping quality and mental well-being.

3. Use the "Budgeting-First" Rule

Always set a strict monthly limit for "wants" vs. "needs". Use a dedicated app or website developed in this study to track your expenses and avoid financial strain.

4. Identify your trigger.

Recognize whether you are shopping because of a genuine need such as a limited-time sale or peer influence. If you feel stressed, opt for "low cost alternative" for coping like exercise or a hobby instead of using shopping as a stress-releif output.

5. Practice Mindful Auditing

Periodically review your purchase history to see if your "behavioral patterns" align with your longterm goals. If you notice your shopping influence shifting from "moderate" to "high", it may be time to consult with a mentor or use the study's suggested self-regulation tools.
