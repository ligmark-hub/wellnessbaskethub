# Healthy Online Shopping Tips

## 1. Set a Clear Budget

Before you even open apps like Shopee or Lazada, decide how much you can spend. Treat online shopping like groceries—planned, not impulsive. If it's not in your budget, it's not in your cart.

## 2. Avoid "Add to Cart" When Emotional

If you're stressed, bored, or sad, pause first. Emotional shopping (retail therapy) can feel good short-term, but it doesn't really solve the problem. Try asking yourself: *Do I need this, or do I just need comfort right now?*

## 3. Wait 24 Hours Before Buying

If it's not urgent, give yourself 24 hours. Most impulsive cravings disappear when you give them time. If you still want it after a day, then it might be a thoughtful purchase.

## 4. Unfollow Tempting Pages

If certain brands or influencers constantly tempt you, mute or unfollow them. Less exposure = less temptation. Your peace and savings matter more than "budol" trends.

## 5. Check Reviews and Ratings

Healthy shopping also means smart shopping. Read reviews, check seller ratings, and compare prices. Don't just trust edited product photos.

## 6. Protect Your Information

Use secure payment methods and avoid sharing OTPs or personal details. Make sure the site is legitimate before entering card details.

## 7. Reflect After Purchasing

After buying something, ask yourself: *Was this worth it? Did it add value to my life?* This helps you become more mindful next time.
